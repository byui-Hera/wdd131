Topside Design – Site Plan

Site Name

Topside Design – Expert Signage Solutions

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the company's specialty in signage and highlights professionalism and top-tier service.

Suggested domain (if available): topsidedesign.com

Site Purpose

This website will act as a digital showcase and client resource for Topside Design. It will highlight the company's services in signage planning, design, fabrication, and installation while providing a contact method and project portfolio to attract and inform potential clients.

Scenarios

  • How can I get a quote for a custom sign for my business?
  • Where can I see examples of signs Topside Design has created for other companies?

Color Scheme

These colors are chosen to represent a bold, professional identity:

  • Purple (#6d2480): Used for accents, buttons, and highlights
  • Dark Gray (#8C8C8C): Page background
  • Black (#cccccc): Main text

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for all headers and section titles
  • Roboto: Used for paragraph text and body content

Both fonts are Google Fonts and selected for their professional and readable appearance.
